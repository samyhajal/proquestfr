    Qui sommes-nous?

    Foundé en 1996, Proquest Engineering has helped customers complete over 100 projects across a wide range of industries worldwide. From medical centers to airports, Proquest Engineering has enabled the completion of many high-profile projects with state-of-the-art MEP designs. Based in Paris and Beirut, we continue to innovate with more sophisticated modeling like 3D BIM.

    We develop engineering and consultancy services with owners, main contractors, architects and real estate program planners. We provide detailed design and execution of prestigious and high standing projects, in various countries in Europe, Middle East, Asia and Africa. We provide design and follow-up of Electrical and Mechanical installations, for private, public and industrial buildings, as well as civil and infrastructure projects.

    En 2018, nous avons été attribués le BIM d'Or, prix organisé par le magazine Le Moniteur, comme coéquipiers de Vinci Construction Grands Projets sur le projet NROC à Astana, Kazakhstan. Ci-dessous les publications relatives à ce prix.
